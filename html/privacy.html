Privacy Policy

Overview

Protecting your data matters to us. This page explains which personal data we collect when you use Join, how we use it, and what rights you have. Personal data means any information that can be used to identify you.

Controller

The controller responsible for data processing is the operator of this application. If you have any questions about how your data is processed, you can contact the address listed in the legal notice at any time.

Data We Process

When you use Join, we process the data you actively provide, such as your name, email address, contact details, and content you store in tasks and notes. We may also process technical data such as IP address, browser type, operating system, date and time of access, and pages visited.

Purposes of Processing

We process your data to provide Join’s features, manage your account, store tasks, and operate the application securely. Technical data helps us maintain stability and security and analyze errors.

Legal Bases

Processing is carried out to perform a contract or pre-contractual measures, and on the basis of our legitimate interest in running a secure and functional application. Where required, we obtain your consent.

Retention Period

We store your data only for as long as necessary for the stated purposes or as required by law. After that, the data is deleted or anonymized.

Data Sharing

We only share data with third parties if it is necessary to provide the application, if we are legally required to do so, or if you have explicitly consented.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and receive your data in a portable format. You can also object to processing and withdraw consent at any time with effect for the future.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy or want to exercise your rights, please contact the address listed in the legal notice.
